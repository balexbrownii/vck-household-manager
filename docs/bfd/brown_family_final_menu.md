## MTHFR REMINDERS

**Always avoid:**
- Enriched flour
- Fortified cereals
- Synthetic folic acid
- Aspartame

**Always prioritize:**
- Natural folate (liver, spinach, lentils)
- Vitamin C (bell peppers, berries, kiwi)
- Quality proteins (grass-fed, wild-caught)

**For Alexander:**
- No leftovers (histamine builds up)
- Cook fresh, eat immediately
- No mandatory cheese

**For Dad (temporary low sulfur):**
- Skip: eggs, garlic, onion, cruciferous
- Most recipes work or have easy swaps
